The problem today

Intake is where referrals go to wait.

Faxes sit. Portals go unmonitored. Eligibility checks happen hours later. Clinical review gets stuck in someone's queue. By the time an acceptable referral gets a yes, the hospital has already placed it with the competition.

Every step is a chance to drop the baton. Every drop is lost revenue, a lost patient, and a damaged relationship with the referring source.

01
Zero leakage of acceptable referrals

Every acceptable referral gets a fast, accurate yes.

Automated insurance eligibility, staffing/coverage checks, and clinical fit screening run within minutes of arrival. No acceptable referral is lost to slow response, a missed fax, or an overloaded coordinator. If an agency can serve the patient, AdmitHub knows it — instantly.

02
One workspace for every admission team

Every team in the admission pipeline lives in AdmitHub.

AdmitHub is the primary — eventually the only — workflow tool for everyone in the admission pipeline: intake coordinators reviewing referrals, account support coordinators chasing missing info from referral sources, clinical reviewers granting admit approval, schedulers routing nurses, and patient welcome call specialists making first contact. As the pipeline expands, so does the platform: embedded calling for source and patient outreach, native scheduling, clinical review queues. One workspace, end to end — instead of five disconnected tools.

03
Minimal human intervention

AI agents do the repetitive work. Humans make the judgment calls.

E&B checks. Staffing availability. Document parsing. AI phone calls to referral sources for missing info. Scheduling and routing. The work that fills a coordinator's day is done in seconds — so coordinators spend their time on the cases that actually need a human.

04
Ecosystem integrations & unified communications

One inbox. Every channel. Full history.

AdmitHub connects into the ecosystem — EMRs, HCHB, Epic, CarePort, Aidin, payer portals, fax, email. Intake staff communicate with referral sources through whichever channel the source prefers, without ever leaving AdmitHub. Real-time portal sync means case manager updates land in AdmitHub the moment they happen — no polling, no refresh, no missed changes.

Just as critically, AdmitHub is wired into every other August product: referral source mappings from the sales tool used by Account Executives, payer network status from RCM, and every adjacent product yet to come. Your team works from one place — and that one place knows everything the company knows.

05
Smart routing & escalation

Escalations land on the right team — with context pre-attached.

Insurance, staffing, and clinical signals pre-scan every referral. When human review is needed, it routes to clinical, auth, staffing, or BD with the full picture already assembled. No "who owns this?" limbo. No hunt for the face sheet.

06
Seamless lifecycle hand-offs

Nothing falls between teams.

Intake → clinical review → scheduling → admission. Every hand-off is structured and loss-less. Ownership and next action are always explicit. If a referral stalls, AdmitHub knows before anyone has to ask.

07
Real-time status for referral sources

No more phone calls asking "what's the status?"

Hospitals, SNFs, and physicians see referral status live: received, accepted, pending, scheduled, admitted, declined-with-reason. Trust builds because they know what's happening without having to chase anyone for an answer.

08
Patient & family experience

The journey, in plain language, from the moment the referral arrives.

Patients and families get proactive updates: "Your referral was received." "Your nurse is scheduled for Tuesday." "Here's what to expect on admission day." Less anxiety. Fewer inbound calls to the agency. Higher NPS — and a reputation that brings the next referral in the door.

09
Compliance & auditability

Every AI decision is explainable and defensible.

HIPAA-compliant by design. A complete audit trail for CMS, payer, and internal audits. Every acceptance or denial comes with a reason a human can read and a regulator can review. Confidence to move fast, because nothing is a black box.
